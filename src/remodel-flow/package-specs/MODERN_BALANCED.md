# Modern — Balanced Package

Internal source-of-truth spec for the **Modern Balanced** curated package.
Documentation only — not wired into UI or engine yet.

---

## Target

- **Finish products budget:** $3,000–$6,000
- **Estimated remodel total (products + labor + shipping):** $12,000–$20,000
- **Style:** Modern (clean lines, matte black hardware, neutral stone/porcelain, warm white lighting)
- **Tier:** Balanced (designer materials with smart trade-offs)

---

## Design intent (whole package)

A bright, architectural Modern bathroom built around a floating vanity, large-format
porcelain stone-look tile, and a unified matte-black fixture suite. Visually quiet,
materially confident. Every bin pulls toward the same palette — warm white walls,
soft grey stone tones, natural oak warmth, matte black metal.

### Cross-bin rules

- **Vanity drives faucet compatibility** — vanity deck/sink type dictates single-hole vs widespread, vessel vs undermount.
- **Mirror + lighting are linked** — sconces flank the mirror; if mirror is backlit LED, sconces are dropped or downgraded to overhead only.
- **Tile must remain coordinated** — wall tile, floor tile, and shower floor tile share a single stone family / tone (warm grey or warm white). No mixing marble look + concrete look + zellige in the same package.
- **Rough-in (valve bodies, drains, supply lines, venting) is handled by the contractor** — not represented as a product bin here.

## Bin: Vanity

**Intent:** Anchor the room with a wall-hung floating vanity in natural oak or matte white. Sets the warmth level for the whole space.

- **Primary:** Floating Oak Vanity, 36" single sink, integrated drawer, matte black pulls
- **Backups (max 3):**
  1. Floating Walnut Vanity, 36" single sink
  2. Floating Matte White Vanity, 36" single sink
  3. Wall-hung Concrete-Top Vanity, 36"
- **Constraints:**
  - Must be wall-hung (floating). No freestanding legs.
  - Single-hole deck cutout to match Modern faucet primary.
  - Width 30–42" only.
  - Hardware: matte black or none (push-to-open).
- **Price range:** $1,200–$2,200
- **Customer-facing text:** "A floating oak vanity that keeps the floor visible and the room feeling open."

- **Primary:** Matte Black Single-Hole Faucet, tall spout

## Bin: Mirror

  2. Backlit LED Mirror, 30"×36" (linked with lighting bin — see constraint)

- **Constraints:**
