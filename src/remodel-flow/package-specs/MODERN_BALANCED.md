## Design intent (whole package)

A bright, architectural Modern bathroom built around a floating vanity, large-format
porcelain stone-look tile, and a unified matte-black fixture suite. Visually quiet,
materially confident. Every bin pulls toward the same palette — warm white walls,
soft grey stone tones, natural oak warmth, matte black metal.

### Cross-bin rules

- **Vanity drives faucet compatibility** — vanity deck/sink type dictates single-hole vs widespread, vessel vs undermount.
- **Mirror + lighting are linked** — sconces flank the mirror; if mirror is backlit LED, sconces are dropped or downgraded to overhead only.
- **Tile must remain coordinated** — wall tile, floor tile, and shower floor tile share a single stone family / tone (warm grey or warm white). No mixing marble look + concrete look + zellige in the same package.
- **Rough-in (valve bodies, drains, supply lines, venting) is handled by the contractor** — not represented as a product bin here.
